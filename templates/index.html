Features of the Concordia Bookstore



Cheaper Prices

Who wants to spend extra money on books, when you could be spending it on tacos? With the cheaper prices of the books bought online, you could save up to $75, or roughly 37 tacos.

Comfortable

Not only can you save money using the CSP Student Bookstore, but you can do it from the comfort of your own home... in sweatpants... while binge watching Netflix. Win-Win.

Completely Student Run

The Concordia Student Bookstore was made by students, for students. Textbooks are expensive, and we all understand the struggle of paying tuition and THEN books. Let your wallet be happy. Join the thousands of students buying and selling on the CSP Student Bookstore.




Textbooks are crazy expensive, amirite?



We think so too. Here at Concordia St. Paul, we take pride in our students' taking the initiative to create a better solution for the insane textbook prices. Whether you are looking to buy, sell, or rent a textbook, the CSP Student Bookstore can take care of you. Our safe, secure forum, lets you interact with other CSP students to ensure you can find what you are looking for.
