Features of the Concordia Bookstore



Cheaper Prices

Who likes to spend extra money on books, when you could be spending it on tacos? With the cheaper prices of the books bought online, you could save up to 75$, which is roughly 37.68 tacos.

Comfortable

Not only can you save money while buying on The CSP Student Bookstore, but you can do it from the comfort of your own home... in sweatpants.. while bingewatching Netflix. Win-Win.

Completely Student Run

Concordia Student Bookstore was made by students, for students. Textbooks are expensive, and we all understand the struggle of paying tuition and THEN books. Let your wallet be happy. Join the thousands of students selling and buying on CSP Student Bookstore.




Textbooks are crazy expensive, amirite?



We think so too. Here at Concordia St.Paul, we pride our students in taking the initiative to create a better solution for the insane textbook prices. Whether you're looking to buy,sell, or rent a textbook, CSP Student Bookstore can take care of you. Our safe, secure forum, let's you interact with other CSP students, to ensure you can find what you are looking for.
